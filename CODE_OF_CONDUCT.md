# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project follows AltmanAI's human-first principles: contributors are expected to engage with honesty, respect, and good faith.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, discussions, and pull requests
- Assume good intent, but hold content to a high standard of accuracy — especially anything touching financial or risk topics
- Disagree on ideas without attacking people

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Deliberately inserting misleading, hype-driven, or advice-like financial content
- Sharing private information (yours or others') without consent

## Enforcement

Violations may result in removal of contributions, comments, or repository access at the maintainer's discretion. Report concerns via the contact method in `SECURITY.md`.
