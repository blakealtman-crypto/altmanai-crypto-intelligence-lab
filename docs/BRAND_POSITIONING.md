# Brand Positioning

## Structure

```
Altman Family Group LLC   (parent company)
        |
     AltmanAI              (technology brand)
        |
        +-- DailyPilot                       (flagship product — human-first productivity AI)
        +-- Sentinel Elite                    (security/vault-style product)
        +-- AltmanAI Crypto Intelligence Lab  (this project — crypto/blockchain research & education)
```

## Why This Distinction Matters

AltmanAI Crypto Intelligence Lab is a distinct initiative under the AltmanAI umbrella, not a rebrand of AltmanAI itself, and not part of the DailyPilot product. Keeping this hierarchy explicit avoids confusing partners, press, or users about what AltmanAI "is" versus what it has built.

## Tagline

**Human-first crypto intelligence, powered by AltmanAI.**

## Tone

Professional, clean, founder-led, technical, trustworthy, compliance-aware, future-focused, human-first. Explicitly not: hype-driven, meme/speculation-focused, or overpromising.
