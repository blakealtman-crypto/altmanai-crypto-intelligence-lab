# AI Usage Policy

This project follows AltmanAI's PAIHI standard — **Proof, Alignment, Integrity, Humanity, Impact** — applied specifically to how AI is used in crypto research and education.

## What AI Is Used For (Current & Planned)

- Summarizing complex blockchain/crypto concepts in plain English
- Drafting educational research notes for human review before publishing
- Helping structure and organize the research library as it grows

## What AI Is Never Used For

- Generating trading signals, buy/sell recommendations, or price predictions
- Automated publishing without human review
- Personalized financial advice of any kind
- Autonomous execution of trades or fund movements (this project has no trading execution capability, by design)

## Human-in-Control Principle

Every piece of AI-assisted content in this project is reviewed by a human (currently, the founder) before publication. AI accelerates research and drafting; it does not have unsupervised authority to publish, advise, or act.

## Alignment With PAIHI

- **Proof** — AI-assisted content should be traceable to its sources where possible.
- **Alignment** — AI output must stay within the educational/research scope defined in `docs/COMPLIANCE_NOTES.md`.
- **Integrity** — no fabricated statistics, fake credentials, or unverifiable claims.
- **Humanity** — the human maintains final judgment and control at every step.
- **Impact** — the goal is genuine understanding, not engagement-maximizing content.
