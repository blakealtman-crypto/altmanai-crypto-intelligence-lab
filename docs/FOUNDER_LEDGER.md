# Founder Ledger

A record of major product decisions, releases, and governance notes for AltmanAI Crypto Intelligence Lab, maintained by Blake Hunter Altman.

---

### Entry 001 — Genesis Alpha (v0.1.0-alpha)
**Date:** 2026-07-06

Founded AltmanAI Crypto Intelligence Lab as the crypto, blockchain, and digital asset research initiative under BlakeAltman Crypto, AltmanAI, and Altman Family Group LLC. Established the founding documentation set (vision, product strategy, compliance notes, risk disclosure, AI usage policy, roadmap, brand positioning), the initial Next.js/TypeScript/Tailwind dashboard architecture, and the compliance-first posture: educational and research only, no trading automation, no wallet custody, no investment recommendations.

**Decision:** Ship as alpha, not beta or v1 — this is a foundation, not a finished product. No monetization, no live financial data, no user accounts in this release.

---

### Entry 002 — Governance Refinement
**Date:** 2026-07-06

Expanded the risk-awareness framework to include Exchange Risk as an explicit eighth category (alongside volatility, liquidity, custody, smart contract, regulatory, emotional decision, and concentration risk). Added this Founder Ledger as a standing governance artifact. Updated roadmap phase structure to explicitly separate the Education Engine (v0.2.0), Risk Awareness Dashboard (v0.3.0), and AI Research Assistant Prototype (v0.4.0) as distinct milestones ahead of Public Research Release (v1.0.0).

**Decision:** License set to MIT for maximum openness at this early research stage.

---

## How Entries Are Added

New entries are appended chronologically, never edited or removed after the fact — this ledger is meant to be an honest history of decisions, not a marketing document. Corrections to a past decision get a new entry referencing the old one, not a silent edit.
