# Product Strategy

## Target Audience

- People curious about crypto/blockchain who want a credible, non-hype starting point
- Existing crypto participants who want a structured risk-awareness framework
- Technical partners, investors, and employers evaluating AltmanAI's execution quality and founder judgment

## Differentiation

Most crypto-adjacent content online optimizes for engagement (price speculation, hype, trading signals). This project optimizes for understanding — glossary-first, risk-first, and explicit about what it is not (a trading tool, a signal service, or investment advice).

## Phased Approach

1. **Genesis Alpha (v0.1.0-alpha)** — foundation: vision, compliance docs, dashboard architecture, glossary/risk frameworks
2. **Content Expansion (v0.2.0)** — deeper research library, more ecosystems covered
3. **AI Assistant Integration (v0.3.0)** — AltmanAI-powered plain-English explanations, human-reviewed
4. **Public Launch (v1.0.0)** — public-facing dashboard, formal compliance/legal review before any expanded claims

## Monetization Posture

No monetization is active in this alpha release. Any future path (e.g., licensing research content, API access, partnerships) will go through explicit legal and compliance review before implementation — see `docs/COMPLIANCE_NOTES.md`.

## Success Signals

- Clarity: can someone with no crypto background understand a core concept after reading one glossary entry?
- Credibility: does the repo read as serious, technically sound, and honest about its current stage?
- Restraint: does every feature stay within the educational/research boundary, with no drift toward trading or advice?
