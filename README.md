## AltmanAI &times; BlakeAltman Crypto

AltmanAI Crypto Intelligence Lab is a research and education initiative from **Blake Hunter Altman**, under **Altman Family Group LLC** and the **AltmanAI** technology brand. It is not a trading bot, a pump project, or a get-rich platform — it is a professional research and intelligence system designed to make crypto, blockchain, and digital asset concepts more understandable, auditable, and responsible.

## Mission

To build a human-first crypto intelligence and blockchain research platform that helps people understand digital assets through transparent AI-assisted education, risk awareness, structured market research, and ethical technology design.

## Product Vision

Most crypto content online is either hype-driven trading noise or dense technical material inaccessible to newcomers. This project builds the middle ground: rigorous, plain-English, risk-aware, and explicit about what it is not. See [`docs/VISION.md`](docs/VISION.md) for the full founder statement.

## Key Features (Alpha)

- **Crypto Education Engine** — a glossary explaining Bitcoin, Ethereum, stablecoins, wallets, exchanges, smart contracts, DeFi, NFTs, tokenomics, liquidity, market cap, volatility, and more
- **Market Intelligence Dashboard** — non-trading, educational market-structure overview by category
- **Risk Awareness System** — 7-category framework: volatility, liquidity, custody, smart contract, regulatory, emotional decision, and concentration risk
- **AI Research Assistant (concept)** — placeholder for future AltmanAI-powered plain-English research summaries, always human-reviewed
- **Digital Asset Research Cards** — structured research topics across major blockchain ecosystems
- **Founder Ledger** — documented product decisions and governance notes (see `docs/` and `CHANGELOG.md`)

## Technology Stack

- **Next.js 14** (App Router) + **TypeScript**
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **React** components, modular and reusable
- No wallet integration, no custody, no automated trading logic in this release — by design
